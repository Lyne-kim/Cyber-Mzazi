How Cyber Mzazi helps

What it does
  • I check messages for unkind words
  • I warn about tricky links that could harm your device
  • I let your grown-up know if you might be in danger
What it does not do
  • I do not read your private diary or photos
  • I do not share your info with strangers or ads
  • I do not secretly record your camera or microphone
